LAKE BEAT FESTIVAL — COMPLETE PRESENTATION

A comprehensive walkthrough of the Lake Beat Festival project

1. THE CONCEPT & PROBLEM STATEMENT

The Challenge: Young people aged 14-20 in Como lack dedicated, safe entertainment venues featuring their preferred music genres (trap and reggaeton). This creates an underserved market gap.

Our Solution: Lake Beat is a professionally-managed music festival offering a secure, high-quality experience tailored to Gen Z preferences. The event combines entertainment with logistical excellence and social responsibility.

2. LOCATION STRATEGY: PIAZZA CAVOUR

Why Piazza Cavour?

    Accessibility: 5-minute proximity to Como Nord Lago train station (eco-friendly transport)

    Safety & Control: Well-defined space enabling effective crowd management and security screening

    Visual Appeal: Stunning Lake Como backdrop perfect for social media virality (Instagram, TikTok)

    Infrastructure: Nearby hotels, parking, hospitals, and police stations ensure operational success

    Community Integration: Historic square enhances prestige and promotes regional tourism

3. MUSICAL PROGRAMMING & MARKET POSITIONING

Trap & Hip-Hop: Dominates Italian streaming charts (Spotify, Apple Music) for ages 14-24. Generates strong FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) and presale conversion.

Reggaeton: International appeal with high dance-factor entertainment value. Attracts diverse audiences beyond dedicated fans, increasing event dwell time and F&B spending.

Lineup Structure: Mix of established headliners and emerging local artists, optimizing cost efficiency while supporting regional talent development.

4A. SECURITY PROTOCOL

Multi-layer screening system. Metal detectors. ID-based access control (age verification wristbands). On-site Advanced Medical Post. Trained stewards and professional coordination with local law enforcement.

4B. STAGE & SETUP

Professional modular stage systems. Anti-panic certified barricades. Clear escape routes per Public Security regulations. Back-of-house and crew areas secured per industry standards.

4C. SUSTAINABILITY (ESG)

Reusable cups on deposit program. Rapid waste disposal via Zero Waste partnerships. Post-event square restoration and cleaning. Environmental responsibility as core brand value.

4D. MARKETING & ACQUISITION

Geo-targeted digital campaigns (Meta & TikTok). Local influencer partnerships. On-campus guerrilla marketing at schools and universities. Press relations and media coverage.

5. REVENUE MODEL & BUSINESS SUSTAINABILITY

Primary Income Streams:

    Ticket Sales: Tiered pricing (Standard, Premium, VIP) maximizes revenue capture across all segments

    Food & Beverage: In-house managed F&B operations with high margin alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages

    Merchandising: Event apparel and collectibles targeting Gen Z consumers

    Sponsorships: Main sponsors (premium brand partnerships) and Technical sponsors (equipment/services providers)

    Partnerships: Hospitality and tourism collaborations with Como hotels and attractions

6A. PROJECT & FINANCE TEAM

Owns executive planning (Gantt charts), Business Plan, budget management, permit acquisition, and municipal negotiations. Ensures on-time, on-budget execution.

6B. MARKETING & BRAND TEAM

Manages Lake Beat's visual identity and brand standards. Creates graphic and video content. Coordinates advertising campaigns. Negotiates sponsor contracts and brand partnerships.

6C. ARTISTIC & OPERATIONS TEAM

Curates artistic programming and Gen Z-aligned content. Manages artist bookings and contracts. Coordinates stage schedule and show operations. Oversees audio, lighting, and front-of-house technical execution.

7. PROJECT TIMELINE & KEY MILESTONES

Q1 2026: Finalize permits, secure headliner artists, launch marketing campaigns

Q2 2026: Confirm sponsor partnerships, begin ticket presales, complete all infrastructure bookings

June 12-13, 2026: Lake Beat Festival execution at Piazza Cavour, Como

Post-Event: Media analysis, ROI assessment, planning for Year 2 expansion

8. SUCCESS CRITERIA & KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

Attendance: 10,000+ attendees across both nights

Safety: Zero to minimal security incidents; 100% incident-free operations

Financial: Break-even or profitable operations; positive sponsor ROI feedback

Experience: High attendee satisfaction (4.5+ net satisfaction rating); strong social media engagement

Community: Positive local media coverage; municipal support for Year 2 and beyond

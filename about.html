THE LAKE BEAT PROJECT

Project Overview & Business Proposition

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Lake Beat is an innovative music festival designed specifically for youth aged 14 to 20. Our event showcases trap and reggaeton music in a safe, organized, and professional environment, created by and for young people.

We are transforming Piazza Cavour—Como's most elegant square—into a vibrant stage for the new generation. Our mission is to deliver a world-class social experience while ensuring complete safety, respecting our community, and boosting youth tourism across the region.

Our Core Values:

  • Safety First: Multi-ring security protocols and professional crowd management
  • Youth-Centered: Programming designed to match Gen Z's musical preferences
  • Sustainable: Zero-waste operations and environmental responsibility
  • Community-Oriented: Supporting local artists and enhancing regional culture

STRATEGIC CHOICE: THE LOCATION

PIAZZA CAVOUR

Como's most iconic square offers the perfect combination of breathtaking aesthetics and logistical feasibility. The area perfectly lends itself to the safe containment and orderly management of large crowds.

Logistical and Strategic Advantages

  • Sustainable Accessibility: Just 5 minutes from the Como Nord Lago train station, encouraging train use and reducing emissions.
  • Flow Management: Large definable space with defined escape routes, ideal for security checks at the gates.
  • Visual Impact: Photographic backdrop on Lake Como, designed to foster virality on social media such as Instagram and TikTok.
  • Infrastructure: Presence of pre-existing logistical support in the surroundings (hotels, parking, nearby Law Enforcement).

MARKET ANALYSIS AND OFFER

The artistic programming is specifically studied on the listening habits of Generation Z to maximize the ticket conversion rate.

TARGET: TRAP & HIP HOP

Dominant genre in the Italian charts (Spotify/Apple Music) for the 14-24 age group. It guarantees strong traction and FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) on presales, especially in the local target.

TARGET: REGGAETON

International appeal and danceable entertainment component. Attracts a heterogeneous audience (not just artist fans), increasing the permanence within the event area and related F&B consumption.

EVENT BUSINESS MODEL

Cost balance between top-tier Headliners and local emerging artists with a lower base cachet. Our revenue streams include tiered ticketing, F&B (managed in-house), merchandising, and sponsorships (Main and Technical partnerships).

OPERATIONAL PLAN AND SECURITY

Logistical pillars aimed at ensuring the total safety and stability of the project in the city context.

SECURITY PROTOCOL

Multi-ring filtering system, specialized stewarding at the gates, metal detectors to prohibit the introduction of weapons/glass, ID checks (differentiated bracelets for no-alcohol minors) and Advanced Medical Post on site.

SETUP

Certified modular stage and anti-panic barricades arranged according to Public Security regulations on large events. Flows, back-of-house, and escape routes clearly traced.

SUSTAINABILITY (ESG)

Exclusive use of reusable cups on deposit. Partnership with waste cycle entities for Rapid Disposal protocol (Zero Waste) and immediate restoration/cleaning of the square post-event.

MARKETING CAMPAIGN

Digital Acquisition through Geo-localized Performance marketing (Meta & TikTok Ads), combined with a PR network for local influencers, and on-field guerrilla activities near high schools and universities.

THE PROJECT TEAM

The complementary skills underlying our organizational Start-Up formed with the project.

Project & Finance Dept

Lead for executive planning (Gantt charts), Business Plan development, cost reporting (budgeting), and mediation with municipal offices for the issuance of permits and licenses.

Marketing & Brand Dept

Owner of the "Lake Beat" format visual identity, creation of graphic and video content, ad coordination, drafting contracts for commercial/technical sponsors.

Art Direction & Ops Dept

Care of the artistic format, artist acquisition (booking), drafting of the stage schedule, stage management, and coordination and alignment for the Audio, Lighting, and FoH sound engineers.

ORAL EXAM PRESENTATION

Social Innovation & Youth Connection

LAKE BEAT FESTIVAL — COMPLETE PRESENTATION

A modern project to reunite the youth of Como through music and innovation

1. VISION & SOCIAL INNOVATION

The Challenge: Young people in Como often feel isolated or have to travel to other cities to find high-quality entertainment and social connection.

Our Innovation: Lake Beat is designed to reunite local youth in their own city. It is a social platform that uses music (Trap and Reggaeton) to foster a sense of community and collective identity for Gen Z.

2. LOCATION & ENHANCED MOBILITY

Piazza Cavour: The heart of Como becomes a hub for connection on June 12-13, 2026.

Transport Strategy: To favor mobility even at late hours, we are organizing dedicated shuttle buses and special transport services. This innovation allows students from the whole province to join the event and return home safely after midnight.

3. MARKETING & COMMUNITY BUILDING

Digital Movement: We are building a community that lives both online and offline. Through viral challenges on TikTok and local influencer partnerships, we connect people before the event even starts.

Direct Engagement: Guerrilla marketing in schools helps us talk directly to our peers, making them the real protagonists of this innovation.

4. FUNDING: TICKETS & SPONSORSHIPS

Accessibility: Our tiered ticket system (Standard, Premium, VIP) ensures that the festival is affordable for every student while maximizing revenue.

Brand Partnerships: We collaborate with local and national sponsors who provide funds and technical equipment. This support is vital to guarantee high-quality production and famous artists without increasing ticket prices.

5A. ESG & RESPONSIBILITY

Innovation means respect for the city. We implement 'Zero Waste' policies and reusable cup programs to ensure that our social gathering has a positive impact on the environment.

5B. SUCCESS & FUTURE

We believe the social impact of reuniting thousands of young people will be the real victory. Lake Beat aims to become a permanent symbol of a more connected and modern Como.
